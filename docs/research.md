# Research

The performance evaluation and design of complex engineering systems,
such as ships and offshore structures, require multidisciplinary
analysis and optimization methods.

The interaction of multiple discipline solvers must be carefully
managed during the design process, while trade-offs among competing
objectives must be considered.

Innovative engineering products operating in real-world environments
require the integration of:

- high-fidelity numerical simulations
- global optimization methods
- uncertainty quantification techniques

The MAO Research Group develops computational methods and architectures
to make multidisciplinary analysis and optimization processes both
accurate and computationally affordable.

Our main application domains include marine and ocean engineering,
ship hydrodynamics, propulsion systems, and offshore structures.
