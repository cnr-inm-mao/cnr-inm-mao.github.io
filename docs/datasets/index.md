# Datasets

## Overview

This section collects curated datasets developed within the MAO research group for:

- design-space dimensionality reduction  
- surrogate-based optimization  
- time-series modeling and system identification  

All datasets are publicly available on :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1} and are part of the MAO research group collection:

👉 https://zenodo.org/communities/cnr-inm-mao/

The collection ensures **consistency, traceability, and long-term accessibility** of all published datasets.

---

## Dataset Categories

### 🔷 Dimensionality Reduction Benchmarks

Datasets designed for **design-space compression**, **PME-based methods**, and **optimization workflows**.

These datasets share a common structure:

- geometry (D)  
- design variables (U)  
- optional physics fields  
- performance outputs  

**Available datasets**

- Bio-inspired underwater glider  
- RAE2822 airfoil  
- DTMB 5415 hull  

👉 [Explore dimensionality reduction datasets](dr.md)

---

### 🌊 Time-Series & Dynamics Datasets

Datasets focused on **temporal evolution of physical systems**, including ship motions and wave-induced responses.

These datasets are suitable for:

- system identification  
- reduced-order modeling  
- machine learning for time-dependent phenomena  

**Available datasets**

- Ship motion time-series dataset  

👉 [Explore time-series datasets](ts.md)

---

## Reproducibility

All datasets include:

- full metadata  
- variable definitions  
- preprocessing details  
- standardized formats  

They are designed for:

- fair benchmarking  
- cross-method comparison  
- integration with PME-toolkit workflows
