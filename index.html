CNR Institute of Marine Engineering

Advanced methods for multidisciplinary analysis and optimization.

The MAO Research Group develops computational methodologies for the analysis, optimization, and design of complex engineering systems, with strong emphasis on marine hydrodynamics, underwater vehicles, propulsion systems, and data-driven engineering design.

Research Scope

The group develops methods that make multidisciplinary analysis and optimization accurate, scalable, and affordable for next-generation engineering products.

Simulation-Based Design Optimization

Design optimization driven by numerical simulation workflows, with emphasis on hydrodynamics, propulsion, and high-dimensional engineering design spaces.

SBDO Hydrodynamics

Multidisciplinary Analysis

Integration of interconnected discipline solvers, fluid–structure interaction, and advanced architectures for complex engineering systems.

MDO FSI

Data-Driven Engineering Design

Surrogate modeling, reduced-order modeling, machine learning, and design-space dimensionality reduction for efficient decision-making.
