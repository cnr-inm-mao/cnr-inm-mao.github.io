    Opportunities¶

    Join the Group¶

    We welcome interest from students, visiting researchers, postdoctoral researchers, and collaborators working on:

    • optimization
    • machine learning for engineering
    • scientific computing
    • marine and aerospace design
    • reduced-order modeling

    Potential Topics¶

    Examples of thesis or research topics include:

    • dimensionality reduction for engineering design
    • multi-fidelity surrogate modeling
    • explainable optimization workflows
    • bio-inspired underwater vehicle design
    • physics-informed machine learning

    How to Apply¶

    Please send:

    • a short motivation message
    • CV
    • research interests
    • optional transcript or portfolio of relevant work

    to the contact listed on the Contact page.
